# Research Protocol

## Research questions

- How often do public nuclear-safety datasets violate their declared freshness?
- Can spatial context distinguish station-local anomalies from network-wide publication failures?
- How stable are schemas, units, and endpoint availability over time?
- How long do public monitoring feeds take to recover after documented weather or seismic events?

## Evaluation

- precision of data-quality findings under manually labeled synthetic faults
- false-positive rate for trajectory deviations
- reproducibility from archived public records
- reviewer agreement
- completeness of evidence packages

No evaluation result should be generalized into a facility safety conclusion.
